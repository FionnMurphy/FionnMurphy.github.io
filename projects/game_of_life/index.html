The Game Of Life



The game of life isn't actually a game. It's a simulation! A board is covered with "cells" that can be "alive" or "dead". Whether or not they come to life depends on how many living cells surround them.

  • 2 or less living neighbours: the cell will die
  • 3 living neighbours: the cell will come to life
  • 4 or more living neighbours: the cell will die
